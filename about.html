About Me

Hi! I'm Sheinele Marfil, 21, from Daanbantayan, Cebu — just a normal human being trying to survive life.

I draw purely as a hobby. I wouldn't call myself a professional or very experienced, but I love learning, improving, and expressing ideas through art at my own pace.

I'm deeply into anime, donghua, manhwas, mangas, and manhuas. These are my daily sources of inspiration, emotional damage, and “one more episode” regrets.

When I'm not drawing, I'm probably listening to music, daydreaming, or staring into space like a loading screen. Music keeps me sane and somehow makes everything feel more cinematic.

Also yes, I'm a gold digger — I like to dig my own gold. Independent, self-made (or at least trying), and powered by effort, matcha, and vibes.
